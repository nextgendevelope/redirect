Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Prompt2Pic Bot?

Prompt2Pic Bot is an AI-based image generation tool that lets users create images by providing a description. The bot uses advanced AI algorithms to generate realistic or artistic images based on the prompts provided by the user.

How do I generate images with the bot?

Simply type a prompt describing the image you want to create, and the bot will generate the image for you. You can also upload your own selfies or create images using predefined styles or themes.
